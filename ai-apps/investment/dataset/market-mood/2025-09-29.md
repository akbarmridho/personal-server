
# Weekly Market Recap (2025-09-29)

## Market Overview

**IHSG** closed **up +0.6% WoW** to **8,099** last week, as the equity index **held its ground despite currency-induced volatility**, with the **Rupiah depreciating against all major and ASEAN currencies**.

However, **blue-chip indexes** declined:

* **LQ45:** -0.9% WoW
* **Sri-Kehati:** -0.8% WoW

This suggests that the **broader Indonesian market** was **once again driven by the conglomerate complex**, rather than fundamentals.

## Outlook: What’s in Store for October?

As we close the **3Q25 season**, most **news and narrative-driven catalysts** will begin to **fade**, as the **major announcements** (e.g., **rate cuts, policy actions**) have already occurred.

### Key Shifts Ahead

* **Investor focus** will shift to **corporate earnings**, expected **end of October**.
* **Banks and cyclicals** are likely to **underperform**, while **select commodities** — particularly **gold** and **CPO** — may **outperform**.

### Macro Indicators

* **Upcoming releases:** Trade (export/import), **PMI**, and **inflation** data this week.
* These are expected to be **non-events**, unless **readings deviate significantly** from expectations.

### Tactical View

Maintain a **“cautious” stance**, as **IHSG trades near its long-term weekly resistance** at **8,100–8,200**.
Expect **conglomerate stocks** to **remain in the driver’s seat**, especially **heading into the MSCI rebalancing in November 2025**.

## Policy Update: Housing Program Initiative

To support the **3 Million Housing Program**, the **Indonesian Ministry of Finance** announced an **interest rate subsidy** for **mortgage loans**:

| Loan Amount         | Annual Interest Rate Subsidy |
| :------------------ | :--------------------------: |
| IDR 10–100 million  |            **10%**           |
| IDR 100–500 million |           **5.5%**           |

This initiative aims to **stimulate housing demand** and **support economic growth** through the **property and construction sectors**.
