Status: RESEARCH
Priority: P3
Effort: Medium

## What Needs Research

- Detection heuristics for PS (Preliminary Support): what volume/price/close-position thresholds distinguish PS from noise and from SC? PS should be softer than SC — moderate volume, buying interest near lows, but not climactic.
- Detection heuristics for PSY (Preliminary Supply): mirror of PS for distribution side.
- How to handle multiple PS/PSY events in sequence (AlphaFlow's BMRI shows "PSY → PSY → PSY → PSY → AR → ST").
- Continuation event: what defines "phase persisted with consistent directional dominance"? How many bars? What dominance threshold?
- Pullback event: what volume decline and price retracement thresholds qualify?
- Validate against AlphaFlow's BMRI phase history examples.
