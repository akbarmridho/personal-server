## Change

Add `memory/notes/agent-performance.md` tracking decision quality over time.

Metrics:

- Correct exits (exit before further decline)
- Missed entries (false WAITs where setup was valid)
- Correct WAITs (setup failed, avoided correctly)
- Entry rate (entries per N desk-checks)
- Cash trend (direction over time)

Behavioral drift flags:

- WAIT duration trending up → entry thresholds may be too restrictive
- Entry rate trending down → systematic under-deployment
- Cash ratio rising for 3+ desk-checks with READY names available → flag and require justification

Updated every desk-check and deep-review.

If false-WAIT rate >50% → note in synthesis that entry thresholds may need recalibration.
If 0 entries for 3+ desk-checks with READY names → flag "systematic under-deployment."
